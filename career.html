Overview

Working at TransForm Solution is an employment experience unlike any other! To be the best, you need the best team behind you, and that is our goal when we initiate the hiring process. Our employees take part in a comprehensive training process that encompasses every element of the industry. When you work at TransForm Solution, you are on the cutting edge of a global industry, so making our employees experts in the field is the only option.

Here’s what to expect when you work at TransForm Solution:

Preliminary Induction Training

From the start, we immerse new employees in our culture with a full day of induction training. This allows you to become acquainted with all aspects of the company, and includes:

  • 1) Information session about the company (history, culture, organizational structure, etc.)
  • 2) Expectations for corporate business etiquette and correspondence.
  • 3) Overview of the services offered by the company.
  • 4) Learning about the different components of outsourcing.
  • 5) Learning the rules of network computer safety.

Positional Introduction Briefing

During the development stage, a manager will go over the obligations, expectations and possibilities, as part of the new employee’s role in the project. Once plans for professional development and growth have been made, a tutor is assigned to help guide and refine the process. This process ensures a smooth transition and helps employees get the most from working at TransForm Solution.

Ongoing Personnel Assessment

In order to plan company activities and initiatives effectively, we conduct regular personnel assessments. During this process, employees receive valuable information on the quality of work they’ve been producing, their strengths and weaknesses, and strategies for moving forward to achieve the highest level of professional growth.

Departmental Rotation

The practice of departmental rotation helps employees gain a greater understanding of the company as a whole as well as providing new opportunities for growth and advancement. Whether it is rotation from one department to another, one project to another or one office to another, employees at TransForm Solution are always given the chance to reach their full potential.
